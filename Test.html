About AMA

Adrian Mortram Associates Limited, AMA (formerly Robin Hume Associates Limited, RHA) is the longest established irrigation design and consultancy practice in the UK and Northern Europe.  Our areas of expertise include golf, sports turf and landscape.  Collectively we have more than a century of experience both nationally and internationally.  The philosophy of the company is to provide impartial, practical and cost effective advice to enable the client to achieve their goals.  We take immense pride in our work, the service we provide to our clients, and in our commitment to environmental conservation ensuring water is used efficiently and effectively.

Our services include irrigation system evaluations, audits, feasibility studies, design, installation monitoring, education and training.

AMA are the appointed specialist irrigation designers and consultants to;

Sports Turf Research Institute (STRI)
St. Andrews Links Trust
Commonwealth War Graves Commission (CWGC)
Motor Industry Research Association (Horiba MIRA)

We also work for the Royal & Ancient (R&A) and the Jockey Club.

We are proud to have maintained a long standing relationship with many of our clients with whom we continue to provide ongoing advice as and when required.

Please feel free to contact us by phone or fill in our contact form to make an enquiry.
